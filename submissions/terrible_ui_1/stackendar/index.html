Stackendar

To select a date and time, stack the scrolling blocks up to the desired month, day, and year. Click on a running board to add a block. Click the "Continue" button to move on to the next board without stacking any more blocks. Don't stack them incorrectly! The selected time will be the time it takes you to complete all three boards. For more information, visit the Stacker Wikipedia page.
